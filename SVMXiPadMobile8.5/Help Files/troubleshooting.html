The Troubleshooting screen allows you to search and access various knowledgebase articles setup in Salesforce. This screen supports presenting rich media content such as video and images. Tap on the numbers below for more information.
Tap to enter a search keyword and tap on the Search button in the keypad. Titles of all matching knowledgebase articles are displayed on the left panel. Tap to view the content of the knowledgebase article. If videos are embedded in the content, tap the play button to play the video. The video can be played in full-screen mode as well. Hold and scroll up/down to read the articleâ€™s contents. Tap on the spinner buttons to navigate between documents.
Rules
All the knowledgebase articles and corresponding keywords are setup online in Salesforce as Documents. To learn more about setting up articles, please refer to the Setup Requirements.
When the screen opens, it automatically performs a search using the product name available in the work order.
Product manual is displayed only if it is setup in Salesforce. To learn more about how to setup product manuals, please refer to the Setup Requirements.
Keywords are comma-separated and can be product names, problem codes and other industry/product specific information.
Since the articles can contain rich media content like streaming video, it can take some time to load on your device, depending on your connectivity speed and strength.
Navigation
Opens the product manual, if available.
Takes you to the Product Chatter screen, if chatter is enabled in your Salesforce.
Returns you to work order details screen.
