Setup Requirements
This screen shows events scheduled on your calendar, and the tasks assigned to you. Tap on the numbers below for more information.
Tap on the required date to see that day's calendar. Use the arrows to switch to a different month. Provides you an alternative way to navigate through the month's days. Tap on it to jump to today's calendar directly. Allows you to view/add tasks to your day's to-do list. Allows you to create a new task. In the popup, enter task description, choose priority and tap Done. Displays the events scheduled for the day. Touch any white space in the schedule area, hold and scroll to see events outside the displayed time window. Tap on an existing event to see the record associated with the event. Hold and drag an event to a different time to reschedule the event. Tap on the ServiceMax logo to know the login user and the App version you are using.
Rules
This screen uses the time zone set on your iPad.
Any changes made to events in iPad are updated to Salesforce immediately when there is connectivity.
The calendar does not synchronize with iCal application.
All-day events, recurring events and multi-day events are not currently supported.
Navigation
Shows all your events scheduled in a week.
Shows the Home screen of the ServiceMax iPad client.
Shows the Map screen with your daily route and allows you to get point-to-point driving directions.
Indicates synchronization completed successfully.
