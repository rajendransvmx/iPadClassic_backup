This screen shows a summary of work performed with all the debrief lines. Using this screen, customers can quickly review the work order cost and approve it by signing off on the details. Tap on the numbers below for more information.
Tap to launch the signature capture panel. Panel to capture signature with watermarking . Tap the Done button after capturing signature. Tap the Cancel button to undo signature capture. Tap the Clear button to clear the signature panel and enter a new signature.
Rules
Every time the debrief information is modified, customer signature needs to be taken again.
Existing service report in Salesforce will be overwritten.
Service report can be generated from this screen only.
