Day View
Setup Requirements
This screen shows the route starting from your home location to all the work orders scheduled for the day back to the home location. If enabled and allowed, it also shows your current location on the map. You can get driving directions from your current location to any work order on the map.
Tap on the numbers below for more information.
Tapping on a purple pin displays a popup. Tapping on the Driving directions button displays the driving directions from your current location to the chosen Work order location. Tap on the red pin to see your home location. Tap on the Job Details button to view Work Order details screen where you can view/edit the work order and enter work. Default map view when there is no connectivity Tap on Driving Directions to view turn-by-turn directions on the bottom left from the current location to the selected work order. Scroll to view the turn-by-turn driving directions for all scheduled work orders. All work orders scheduled for the day are displayed here. Tapping on the work order will show the brief details at the bottom right section. Tap on the Job Details button to view Work Order details screen where you can view/edit the work order and enter work.
Rules
At least one Work Order must be scheduled in the day to view the map.
Invalid addresses in work orders will result in error and will not be plotted on the map.
Pins plotted on the map are color-coded. Blue indicates your current location, Red indicates your home address configured in Salesforce and Purple indicates work order events.
The route is plotted in the order of how work orders appear in the calendar.
Your home/starting address can be changed only in Salesforce online.
If there is no connectivity, map will not be plotted and displays blank screen with a message
Navigation
Returns you to the screen from which you opened map view.
