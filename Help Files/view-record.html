Setup Requirements
This screen presents a Salesforce record with its child records in a view configured by your administrator using SFM Transaction Designer. Same type of record can have more than one type of view applicable.
Tap here to switch to a different view of the work order. Tap here to view all header sections. Tap the header section name to see the information from the section only. Tap the child section name to see the information from child records, and it may also include related objects. Displays the Sync status. Double tap here to drill down and see the respective SFM view. Tap on the dotted label to see full label. Tap on the dotted data to see full data. Tap one of these SFW actions as applicable to the business process. Tap here to download more Product history and Account history respectively. Displays conflict message. Displays a timestamp of the latest refresh.
Rules
The layout, section and fields in this view is fully configurable using SFM Transaction Designer.
You can use only those views for which your administrator has given access.
Special custom options such as Troubleshooting, Service Report, and Chatter can be enabled for Work Orders only.
'See More' works only when there is connectivity.
Navigation
Shows the custom actions such as SFM Transactions configured by your administrator.
Takes you to the screen from which you opened this record for view.
Allows you to capture an approval signature on iPad and save it as an attachment to this record in Salesforce.
Indicates synchronization completed successfully.
Indicates synchronization in progress.
