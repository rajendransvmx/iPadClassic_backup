Tap on the relevant text field to launch the keyboard. Tap here to launch the barcode scanner. Tap here to perform a lookup using the scanned barcode data.
Rules
The following barcode formats are supported:
ITF, QR, UPC-E, UPC-12 (UPC-A), CODE39, CODE128, EAN8, EAN13.
Scanning devices can be configured via Bluetooth. To know more, refer to device's/iPad's documentation.
Scanning might not be successful for fuzzy/damaged barcodes or under low light. Refer to the Barcode Reader Help by tapping on the Info icon on the barcode capture screen.
Support for Barcode scanning is available only from ServiceMax Summer 12 release.
